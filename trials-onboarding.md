# Getting started with trials
{: #trial-onboard}

Thank you for choosing {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}}. We are excited to support your journey as you explore the trial experience.

## What to expect during your trial
{: #trial-whatto-expect}

During your trial of {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}}, you will have the opportunity to explore the following key capabilities:

   - **Seamless integration**: Discover how {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}} can be efficiently integrated into your existing IT infrastructure to support your storage needs.


   - **Block storage provisioning and consumption**: Provision and attach a block volume from IBM Storage Ceph as a Service to deploy and run a sample workload.


        The sample workload will be deployed in a VMware environment using MongoDB, which consumes the provisioned block volume.
        {: note}

   - **Object storage usage**: Create and manage object storage buckets to store and access data using {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}}.


        This trial provides a functional experience of {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}} using virtualized resources that simulate a production environment. The performance observed during the trial may differ from that of a full-scale production deployment.
        {: note}

## Begin your trial onboarding
{: #begin-trial-onboarding}

After you opt in to the {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}} trial, you will receive an email invitation to join IBM Cloud.

1. To proceed, click **Join now** in the trial invitation email with the subject line **"Welcome to IBM Storage Ceph as a Service remote trials."**

2. Ensure that you use the same email address that received the invitation. If you are using a Gmail address, you may choose the **Sign up with Google** option on the sign-up page.

3. Review and accept the terms, then click **Join account** to complete your registration.

You are now successfully enrolled in the {{site.data.keyword.cephaas_full_notm}} trial on IBM Cloud.
