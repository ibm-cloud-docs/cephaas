# Viewing cluster details
{: #view-cluster-details}

Open cluster details to review the current status, storage information, and health information for a cluster in your Fusion as a Service deployment.
{: shortdesc}

## Before you begin
{: #view-cluster-details-prereqs}

Before you view cluster details, make sure that you can open the deployment in the {{site.data.keyword.cloud_notm}} console.

## Open cluster details
{: #access-cluster-details}

1. Open your Fusion as a Service deployment.
2. Click the **Clusters** tab.
3. In the cluster list, click the cluster name.

The cluster details page opens for the selected cluster.

## Information shown on the cluster details page
{: #cluster-details-sections}

The cluster details page can include the following information:

- Cluster name and other identifying details.
- Fusion services status.
- Storage quota and storage usage, if Fusion services are enabled.
- Warning, alert, or health information.
- Actions that are available for your role.

## Why to use cluster details
{: #available-actions}

Use the cluster details page to do the following tasks:

- Review whether Fusion services are enabled for the cluster.
- Check storage quota and storage usage.
- Review warnings or alerts for the cluster.
- Open related actions such as storage management or the OpenShift console, if those actions are available for your role.
